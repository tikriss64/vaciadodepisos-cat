Clear answers

Frequently asked questions for international owners

Can I manage the entire process without travelling to Spain?

+

Yes. Most of our clients live abroad. We can coordinate the complete clearance, item cataloguing and key handover without you having to travel. We maintain constant communication via email, phone or video call.

How do you guarantee discretion in private estates like Can Girona or Vallpineda?

+

We adapt to each community's protocols. We only access during authorised hours, use unmarked vehicles and maintain a smooth relationship with private security and local administrations. Discretion is an essential part of our service.

What happens to personal items or sensitive documents?

+

We sort them manually and meticulously. Before removing any item, we send photos and lists for your approval. Personal documents are handed over directly to the owner or securely destroyed, according to your instructions.

What about items I want to keep?

+

We can identify, classify and prepare them for collection. If you need them shipped to another country or stored temporarily, we advise on the safest options in Sitges and Barcelona, and coordinate delivery with the company of your choice.

Can you coordinate with my lawyer, notary or estate agent?

+

Of course. We are used to working with legal representatives, property managers and estate agents in Sitges and Garraf. We can hand over keys, sign access forms and prepare the property for sale or rental.

How does payment work from abroad?

+

We issue a detailed invoice and accept SEPA transfers from any European country. We do not work with cash. Transparency is absolute at every stage of the process.
